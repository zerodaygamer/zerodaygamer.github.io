I decided to enroll to courses like PMRP and GREM to gain some knowledge about reverse engineering and earn a certification along the way. In addition to enrolling to some courses, I am also following game hacking guides on GuidedHacking.

Certification alone is not enough to get into the Reverse Engineering field, I proved that, even an HR interview invitation is rare for me. I though having a lot of certification on my bag will be an easy HR interview invitation but I’m wrong. So, I decided to create this blog to document my process towards becoming a good reverse engineer. Hope this works!!
